Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our landscaping services in Sydney.

How much does landscaping cost in Sydney?

Landscaping costs vary based on project size, materials, and complexity. For Sydney homeowners, basic garden makeovers start around $5,000, while comprehensive landscape designs with hardscaping typically range from $15,000 to $50,000+. We offer free consultations to provide accurate estimates for your specific project.

What native plants work best in Sydney gardens?

We recommend Sydney-appropriate natives like:

  • Coastal areas: Banksia integrifolia, Lilly Pilly, Pig Face
  • Shady gardens: Ferns, Native Violet
  • Full sun: Kangaroo Paw, Grevilleas, Bottlebrush
  • Low-water: Lomandra, Dianella, Westringia

Our designers will select plants suited to your specific microclimate and soil conditions.

How long does a landscaping project take?

Project timelines depend on scope, weather, and approvals needed. Typical durations:

  • Small garden refresh: 1-2 weeks
  • Backyard makeover: 3-6 weeks
  • Complete property landscaping: 6-12 weeks
  • Commercial projects: Varies by size

We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated on progress.

Do you handle council approvals for landscaping?

Yes, we manage the entire approval process for projects requiring council consent, including:

  • Tree removal applications
  • Retaining walls over certain heights
  • Fencing near boundaries
  • Heritage-listed property modifications

We're familiar with Sydney's various council requirements and can advise what approvals your project may need.

What's your service area in Sydney?

We serve all of Greater Sydney, with particular focus on:

  • Northern Beaches (Manly to Palm Beach)
  • Eastern Suburbs (Bondi to La Perouse)
  • North Shore (Mosman to Hornsby)
  • Inner West (Balmain to Strathfield)

Contact us to confirm if we service your specific suburb.
