About Twisted Landscapes

Twisted Landscapes is a Sydney-based landscaping company dedicated to creating sustainable, beautiful outdoor spaces. Our team brings passion and expertise to every project, designing gardens that thrive in Sydney's unique climate.

We specialize in eco-friendly solutions, from native plant gardens to low-maintenance hardscapes, serving homeowners and businesses across Sydney.

Why Choose Us?

  • Sustainable, eco-friendly practices
