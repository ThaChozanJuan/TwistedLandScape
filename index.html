More Than Landscapers – We’re Outdoor Designers

Led by Sean Kellendonk, Twisted Landscapes is a Northern Beaches-based team that turns tired yards into stunning, sustainable outdoor spaces. We specialise in design-first landscaping that’s built to last and thrive in Sydney’s unique coastal climate.

From lush native gardens in Seaforth to low-maintenance terraces in Newtown, we deliver creative, practical landscapes that blend form and function — and we do it with heart, precision, and plenty of local knowledge.

Servicing the entire Sydney region — because good landscaping should never be limited by postcode.
